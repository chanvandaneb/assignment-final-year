FAQs

Q: How do I check the status of my order?

A: Upon placing your order, please allow up to 3-5 business days to have the order processed. Our fulfillment warehouse packs your goods and has it shipped as soon as possible. Once your order has been shipped, you will receive a tracking number to your order. You may also come back to our website at any time and visit our order status page to find your real-time update on your shipment. If you did not receive your tracking number or have any updates on our Order Status page after 5 business days, please contact us immediately and we will do all we can to investigate.

Q: How long does shipping take?

A: Our warehouse is located around the world. For domestic order in the United States, It will normally take 14 to 25 business days. For international orders, please allow up to 30 days.

Q: Do you ship Internationally?

A: Yes! We ship worldwide and ensure we make ever fashion lovers an enjoyable shopping experience with us.

Q: Is your website secure with my personal information?

A: Absolutely! We use SSL security to ensure all your personal information is encrypted. We do not store your credit Card information and it will only be used one-time only upon purchasing your product. Your credit Card information will be purged.

Q: How do I order on your website?

A: Simply find the product you'd want click the blue "Add To Cart" button. fill in your shipping and billing information and we'll have your order shipped to you as soon as possible!

Q: Does Arura® provide a quality guarantee for all the products being sold?

A: Absolutely! If you do not like the product or find damages to the product contact us immediately! We do all we can do ensure your best shopping experience. You can simply contact us and we'll get it addressed!

Q: Do you offer a refund if I don't like the product?

A: Absolutely! We offer a full refund on your order if you don't like the product. simply visit our refund policy page for complete details.
